## Purpose

Track people and teams to contact for:
- discovery interviews
- lightweight feedback asks
- demo reviews
- design-partner conversations
- future pilot conversion

## Outreach sequencing rule

Start with:
1. warm internal contacts
2. warm external or semi-warm ecosystem people
3. cold but high-fit external people

---

## Ask ladder

Use the smallest ask first.

1. “Would you be open to reacting to a 60-second demo?”
2. “Would you give me 2–3 brutally honest sentences on whether this feels compelling?”
3. “Would you do a 15-minute feedback call?”
4. “Would you be open to a design-partner style working session?”
